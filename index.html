How can help ?

Quantitative Analysis and Business Development.

Through building market linkages, or helping you make sense of the data that holds insights to the decisions that will drive the outcomes that matter most for your business, I will help set you apart from the rest. And because I can self direct and have a solid base in project management, you can focus on bigger things while I handle these.

How can help ?

Fullstack Web and Android Development.

Beyond writing code in several programming languages, you will be able to accelerate development on both you web and mobile teams through a developer who can translate with much ease business problems into programmable technical solutions, and speed up how you navigate the ambiguity that exist in solution design.

Why go with me ?

Outside getting the technical right, here are five more features that will make your experience of me mouth watering.

Versatile Problem solving

Imagine a problem solver who understands how to tackle challenges on both the business end and the technical end of your organization, and who can shift across at least five teams and still create value effortlessly.

Experience in partnership development

With over three years of experience building partnerships at AIMS, you will be getting talent that can identify and build the relations that help drive the outcomes you seek.

Advanced procedural thinking

Leaning on over ten years of graduate and postgraduate mathematics training, you be able to accelerate the way you develop programming solutions by leveraging my well formed ability to think procedurally, and stress test what we know.

Effective communication

Imagine what you can do across multiple teams with my ability to effectively communicate technical details to a non-technical audience, and break down complex stuff to bite size pieces everyone can swallow.

Ease with ambiguity

Before we think of what a feasible solution to a problem is, we need to understand the problem, and this takes even more time than designing the solution. The extra value you get with me, is that I can make this faster.

Problem solving is two things : goal and strategy.

For every problem, I set the objectives, and the key results, then complete the road map to a sturdy solution with an outline of the strategy.

This allows me to remove ambiguity from the problem case definitions, focus on exactly what we want to achieve, and underline what our non-negotiable outcomes are.
